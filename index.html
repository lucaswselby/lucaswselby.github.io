I am a skilled writer and editor with a Bachelor's degree in Creative Writing from the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences at Arizona State University. I have experience with academic writing through my position as a Writing Mentor for the College of Integrative Sciences and Arts at Arizona State University, creative writing through my position as a script editor at High Entertainment and Fiction Editor at Radon Journal, and commercial writing through my position as an Office Manager at Arizona Fine Violins. I prefer to tackle projects head-on and am not afraid to work alone or cohesively with a group either as a member or as a leader. I am currently open to freelance work in writing and editing for any field.
